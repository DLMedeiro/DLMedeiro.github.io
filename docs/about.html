About Me

Hi my name is Danielle.

Certified Software Engineer

Since my first role as a project engineer, I began envisioning my professional journey as a progression towards my ultimate dream job. With each new initiative, I could build and improve upon the current state. As my career advanced, I eventually found myself confined to a narrow path, with little enthusiasm for the multiple careers within reach. That's when it hit me—I had made a mistake, and I am now on a mission to embark on a fresh path.

If I possessed the ability to turn back the hands of time, I would have opted to major in Computer Science with a minor in Psychology and Design.

My career aspirations are to work on challenges that will keep my mind engaged and make me come back for more. I want “geeking” out on my work to feel effortless. Most importantly, I want to make a tangible impact on the problems in the world that truly matter to me—the ones that often feel like a lost cause.
