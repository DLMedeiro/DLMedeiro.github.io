About Me

Hi my name is Danielle.

I am a certified Software Developer with a passion for frontend development and quality assurance. I also hold a B.S. degree in Industrial Engineering, which has equipped me with a strong foundation in problem-solving and analytical thinking.

Through various roles, I have had the opportunity to explore multiple facets of the tech industry, gaining valuable insights and expanding my skill set along the way.

My long-term career goals revolve around continuous growth, both professionally and personally. I am committed to building a meaningful and fulfilling career, one that goes beyond just a job. Specifically, I am drawn to making an impact in either the healthcare or education industries, as I believe technology has the power to transform and improve these sectors.

I thrive in environments where I can immerse myself in projects, constantly learn new things, and find creative solutions that positively impact others. In my previous role, I collaborated closely with Software Engineering teams, contributing to the design, testing, and implementation of strategic solutions tailored to meet unique business needs. Having previously worked in manufacturing, I deeply understand the critical role that quality plays in the successful execution of any product. I have conducted in-depth analyses of operational processes, uncovering root causes of issues and inefficiencies. This enabled me to implement effective problem-solving strategies and drive continuous process improvement. Additionally, I facilitated collaborative discussions between business stakeholders, proposing and documenting solutions that enhanced operational effectiveness and compliance.

With my blend of industrial engineering expertise and software development skills, I am ready to take on new challenges, contribute to impactful projects, and make a difference in the tech industry. I am excited about the prospect of joining a team where I can apply my knowledge and collaborate with like-minded individuals to create innovative solutions that positively impact the world around us.
